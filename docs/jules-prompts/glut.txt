4. `grep -rn "fetch\|sql\|db\." b-content/worker/routes --include="*.ts"` — DB call patterns
5. `grep -rn "React.memo\|useMemo\|useCallback" b-content/src --include="*.tsx" | wc -l` — memoization usage
